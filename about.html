Chisuma is a small village 25km east of Victoria Falls. Perched on the escarpment of the Mighty Zambezi River the village is inhabited by 3000 - 4000 local villagers. Such diversity of tribes within the area provides a potential of developing Chisuma village into a food basket for hotels, lodges and other entities within Victoria Falls.

The name ‘Chisuma’ is derived from an indigenous fruit tree bearing a sweet taste common in the area, elders say it means a very big “chi“ tree of that species (called unsuma).

At Chisuma Foundation, wellbeing is at the forefront of what we are working towards. Our programs and activities are designed to connect and accelerate ambitions into reality. Learn more about our daily positive impact to the community. We invite you to join us to generate positive change.

Mission

Youth and Talents is a progressive youth organization working to build a movement of resilient and empowered youths through the provision of skills and talent development career, education assistance and youth advice, representation, sustainable income-generating activities mainly agricultural oriented, adaptation of climatic change response strategies, and advocacy for systemic change.

Vision

We envision a community where every youth is empowered. We will have an expanded partnership and funding base that sustains the current operations and supports anticipated program growth.

Values

Committed to achieving results in pursuit of our vision and mission Collaborative because we are better together Accountable to the community, our constituents and each other Adaptive to change, because we take the long view

Objectives

To set up sustainable projects for the youths; To provide psychosocial support to youths in marginalized communities To identify and develop youth talents
